Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Fostering a culture of creativity and technological advancement. Making Bangladesh a hub for innovation.

Sustainability

Promoting eco-friendly practices and renewable energy sources. Ensuring a sustainable future for Bangladesh.

Infrastructure

Building a resilient and sustainable infrastructure for the future. Ensuring access to quality infrastructure for all.

Empowerment

Empowering citizens through education and technology. Ensuring equal opportunities for all.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future for the people.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

“Bangladesh’s future lies in empowering every citizen, fostering innovation, and embracing sustainability. By investing in education, technology, and social business, we can create opportunities for all. Together, let us build a nation where prosperity is shared, the environment is protected, and hope thrives. Bangladesh 2.0 will be a model for inclusive, sustainable development.”


Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Advisor of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
